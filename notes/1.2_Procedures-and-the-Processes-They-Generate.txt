1.2.1 Linear Recursion and Iteration

Factorial
n! = n*(n-1)!
1! = 1

recursive version:
(define (factorial n)
  (if (= n 1)
       1
       (* n (factorial (- n 1)))))

Iterative version
(define (factorial n)
    (fact-iter 1 1 n))
(define (fact-iter product counter max-count)
    (if (> counter max-count)
        product
        (fact-iter (* counter product)
                    (+ counter 1)
                    max-count)))
